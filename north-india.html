LET'S EXPLORE NORTH INDIA

North India is a region of diversities which include different climate, language, costume, culture and many geographical differences. It consists of snow capped Himalayan peaks along with hill stations at highest to low altitudes. North India also has Thar Desert in the state of Rajasthan.

Famous Spots

SHIMLA

Key Attractions:
Jakhoo Hill, and Scandal Point
Things To Do:
Trekking, paragliding
Best Restaurants:
Cafe Sol, 45 the Central
Best Time To Visit:
November and February

VARANASI

Key Attractions:
Dashashwamedh Ghat and Vishwanath Temple
Things To Do:
Visiting major tourist attractions
Best Restaurants:
Varanasi cafe & restaurant
Best Time To Visit:
November to March

LADAKH

Key Attractions:
Nubra Valley, Stakna Monastery
Things To Do:
Paragliding, trekking, snow sledding
Best Restaurants:
Jalsa, Unlocked restaurant
Best Time To Visit:
April to June

Udaipur

Key Attractions:
Jagdish Temple, City Palace, Lake Pichola
Things To Do:
Boating, sightseeing, shopping
Best Restaurants:
Tribute Restaurant, Jhumar Restaurant, Sunset View
Best Time To Visit:
September to March
